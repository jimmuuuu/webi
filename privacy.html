Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Webi ("we", "us") operates webi.it.com. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it. The short version: we only collect what you choose to send us, and we never sell it.

Information we collect

  • Contact form submissions. When you fill out our contact form, we receive the details you provide — your name, business name, email address, phone number, and project description.
  • Nothing else. We don't run analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or set cookies on this site.

How we use it

We use your contact details for one purpose: responding to your inquiry and, if you hire us, delivering your project. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing.

Form processing

Our contact form is delivered to our inbox by FormSubmit, a third-party form service. Your submission passes through their servers in order to reach us; see their privacy policy for details on their handling.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard technical data (such as IP addresses) for security and operational purposes, as described in GitHub's documentation.

Your choices

Want us to delete an inquiry you sent us? Email webidesignedit@gmail.com and we'll remove it.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update this page and the date above.
